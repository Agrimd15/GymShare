Web Dashboard

An intuitive online dashboard allows users to post and claim ads for home gyms as well as track their fitness progress.

Mobile App

The mobile app provides on-the-go access to the dashboard for quick updates on equipment status and easy creation of workout plans. It also provides updates by text through the Twilio API.

Physical Fitness Tracker

We also include a physical hardware fitness tracker to make tracking and measuring workout effectiveness and meeting goals more simple for the average user. It gives live information on calories burnt and other essential factors in physical fitness.
